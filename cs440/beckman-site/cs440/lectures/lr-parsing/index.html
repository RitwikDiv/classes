LR Parsing

Tuesday, June 23, 2015

LR parsers have been the industry standard for quite some time. The gnu C compiler even comes with an LR parser generator (called bison, a take-off of an older utility called yacc. Computer people like puns.). These parsers are fast and capable, but to be effective using them you really need to understand how they work.
