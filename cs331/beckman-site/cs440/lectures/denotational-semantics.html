Denotational Semantics

Tuesday, July 07, 2015

In the [operational semantics][opsem] lecture you saw how to model a program by showing what one step of computation does, and combining the results. In dentational semantics we give a mathematcal meaning for every expression, and show how to combine these to determine the meaning of larger expressions.
